About Me

Hello! My name is Tatsuki. Since childhood, I've been fascinated by the mysterious power of words, and I've been learning various languages and exploring their depths. This blog is a personal record of my language learning journey, and I started it with the desire to share the wonders of the world seen through language and the possibilities of communication with all of you.

As a hobby, I'm dedicated to multilingual learning, with a particular focus on English, Russian, and Kyrgyz. I'm also interested in Classical Japanese, which appears in classical literature, and the creation of artificial languages. I believe that language is not just a series of symbols, but something that shapes culture, history, and human thought itself.

I also love traveling, and the languages and people I've encountered in various countries have deepened my curiosity. Sometimes, I even consider mathematics and music as "languages," exploring their commonalities and differences. I hope this blog sparks your interest in languages and becomes a catalyst for new discoveries.

Please take your time to join me on my "journey through words."
